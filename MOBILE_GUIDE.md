# 📱 CashFlow Predictor Pro - Mobile Guide

## How to Use on Mobile

### Option 1: PWA (Recommended - Free & Easy)

1. Open the app in Chrome/Safari on your phone
2. Tap the **Share** or **Menu** button
3. Select **"Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install App"**
4. The app will appear like a native mobile app

### Option 2: Bookmark

1. Bookmark the app URL
2. Access quickly from your home screen bookmarks

### Option 3: Native Mobile App (For Full Native Experience)

For a true native mobile app, consider:

| Platform | Service | Cost |
|----------|---------|------|
| iOS/Android | FlutterFlow | Free tier |
| iOS | BuildFire | $$ |
| Both | Glide | Free tier |
| Both | thunkable | Free tier |

## Deployment Options for Mobile App Store

### Google Play Store / Apple App Store

To publish to app stores, you need:

1. **Convert to Native**: Use Flutter, React Native, or Kotlin
2. **Wrap with WebView**: Services like Capacitor or Cordova
3. **PWABuilder**: Microsoft's free PWA-to-app conversion tool

### Recommended Conversion Tools

| Tool | Description | Cost |
|------|------------|------|
| [PWABuilder](https://pwabuilder.com) | Convert PWA to iOS/Android | Free |
| [FlutterFlow](https://flutterflow.io) | No-code mobile builder | Free tier |
| [Glide](https://glideapps.com) | Convert to mobile app | Free tier |
| [Thunkable](https://thunkable.com) | Cross-platform builder | Free tier |

---

## Quick Setup: PWABuilder

1. Deploy to Streamlit Cloud
2. Go to [pwabuilder.com](https://pwabuilder.com)
3. Paste your app URL
4. Download iOS/Android packages
5. Submit to respective app stores
